Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and login credentials
  • Location data (including background location during active attendance sessions)
  • Camera input (facial images) for attendance verification

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To provide attendance marking functionality
  • To verify user identity using face verification
  • To track attendance and ensure worksite compliance using location services

3. Location Data

Our app collects location data only during an active attendance session initiated by the user after marking attendance.

  • Location is captured at the time of attendance check-in using face verification
  • Periodic location checks are performed approximately every 30 minutes during active sessions
  • Used to verify user stays within authorized 200-meter work radius
  • Background location tracking stops automatically after check-out

Location data is not used for advertising, profiling, or marketing and is not shared with third parties.

4. Face Data (Face Detection and Verification)

  • Facial images are captured only at the time of attendance marking
  • Used strictly for real-time identity verification
  • Raw facial images are not stored on the device
  • Encrypted biometric templates may be generated for authentication
  • Templates are securely stored on backend servers
  • Face data is not shared with any third parties

Face data is used only for attendance and identity verification and not for advertising, profiling, or tracking.

5. Data Retention

  • Face data is retained only while the user is active in the organization
  • Deleted permanently upon account deactivation or termination
  • Other data retained only as necessary for service and legal requirements

6. Data Storage

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit
  • Encryption at rest
  • Role-based access control

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data.

Data may only be shared:

  • Within the organization (role-based access)
  • When required by law

Face data is never shared with third parties.

8. Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access or misuse.

9. Account Management

Accounts are created by organization administrators. Users do not register independently.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
